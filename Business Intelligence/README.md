From the Business Intelligence course, valuable knowledge and practical skills related to data analytics, visualization, and decision support systems were developed. The course provided exposure to various Business Intelligence concepts such as data warehousing, ETL processes, dashboard development, predictive analytics, and data storytelling. Through hands-on laboratory sessions and projects using industry-standard tools including Alteryx, Tableau, and Power BI, practical experience was gained in data cleaning, transformation, visualization, and dashboard design. In addition, the industry talk and project-based learning activities enhanced understanding of how Business Intelligence solutions are implemented in real business environments to support data-driven decision-making. The course also strengthened analytical thinking, problem-solving, communication, and teamwork skills through collaborative projects and presentations. Overall, this course successfully improved both technical competency and the ability to transform raw data into meaningful insights for business applications.
