From the Entrepreneurship and Innovation course, valuable understanding of entrepreneurship concepts, business planning, and innovation strategies was developed. The course provided practical exposure to generating business ideas, developing a Business Model Canvas (BMC), and conducting real business activities related to Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). Through the group project, experience was gained in planning, managing, and executing an actual business operation using social media platforms such as Instagram and TikTok for marketing and promotion. In addition, skills in communication, teamwork, leadership, problem-solving, and time management were strengthened throughout the project development, pitching session, and final presentation. The course also improved creativity and critical thinking through AI-assisted idea generation and real-world business decision-making processes. Overall, this course provided meaningful hands-on entrepreneurial experience and increased understanding of how innovative business ideas can be transformed into practical business activities.
